Food Safety

This website provides inspection reports for permitted establishments regulated by the Prince George County Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services' Division of Food Safety.

Food safety is a global issue that concerns every living person. Everyone has to eat in order to sustain themselves, so it is very important that people are aware of if their food is safe to eat. With this application, we aim to enable the general public of Prince George's county to search for food safety ratings of various local restaurants. We hope that this site will be used to educate and inform people of any potential restaurants to steer clear of to prevent health complications.
