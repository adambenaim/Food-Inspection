About

Our group is comprised of Information Science undergraduate students at the University of Maryland. We all care about food safety/public health, and we wanted to create an application that would enable our local community to become more well-informed as to where their food comes from and how safe it is to eat. We wholeheartedly believe that this application will do our fellow students a service, and hope that they will use it to become more conscious of where their food comes from.
